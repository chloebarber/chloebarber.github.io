About Me

I became a software engineer because I am a firm believer in the “work smarter, not harder” ethos. At my previous positions in Technical Support, my favorite part of the job were the opportunities I got to focus on process improvement. I regularly wrote macros in both AutoHotKey and Excel Visual Basic to not only speed up the rate at which I was able to process tickets (especially given some of the legacy software we had to rely on!), but also to make my work experience more enjoyable in and of itself. When tasked with doing preliminary market research in my downtime, I taught myself basic PHP and wrote my own webcrawler to be spared the tedium of data entry - and turned in the results on time, too!
